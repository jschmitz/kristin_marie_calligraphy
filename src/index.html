I began writing in italics when I was eight years old. Growing up in the South, my grade school required an italic penmanship class every morning from the third grade through the eighth grade. Who knew that more than twenty years later I would still be writing in calligraphy...and enjoying it!

Primarily, I have addressed wedding invitations, bridal shower invitations and wedding place cards. However, if you have something else in mind, run it by me. I am always looking for unique ways to add that special touch with handwritten calligraphy.

After all, people notice the small things, and calligraphy gets noticed.

I live and work in Chicago. But I have been asked to do calligraphy for events everywhere - from Annapolis, MD to Tampa, FL to Sydney, Australia. Please contact me to ask questions or request a quote.
